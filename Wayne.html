Hi, I am Wayne Hastings

Wayne Hastings is an arts management student at Indiana University. He enjoys looking at art, playing guitar, and visiting museums. Hastings is especially passionate about Indiana culture and history. His long term career goal is to work for a museum or historical center as a collections manager.

About me

Passion

My greatest passion is learning about Indiana culture and history. Indiana is my home and is the reason I want to work for a cultural institution. I want to help share and preserve the art and traditions that come from this part of the world. Community radio is another passion of mine, having hosted a radio show on both WFHB and WIUX. I often love broadcasting rhythm & blues, early jazz, and country music.
