German Equestrian Summer Camp

Ismail Racecourse is Europe's largest thoroughbred Arabian racecourse. It is located in the picturesque 200-hectare private estate of the Schneider Zoo in Germany. The owner of the racetrack comes from a famous equestrian family with more than 300 years of Equestrian experience. The racecourse transports excellent breeds of Arabian horses to significant events every year and has won numerous awards. The horses it has bred have been purchased by Arab chiefs and given to the Chinese leader Xi Jinping as a gift.

The racecourse also provides customized equestrian courses for students of all ages and levels. The standard coursework unfolds over 6 days, including 5 days of equestrian classes and one day of cultural activities. The specific content can be customized according to the student’s needs.
