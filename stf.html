Stanford Technology Summer camp

The Stanford Summer Camp is designed for students who love innovation and are willing to explore the unknown. The summer camp will be based on learning from outstanding people in their professions, developing a vision for the future, and will focus on artificial intelligence, VR / AR, robotics, 3D printing and other related aspects of such.

For two weeks, students will not only receive in-person guidance from the world's top professors, but also experience the charm of North America's top summer camp with students from all over the world. There will also be seminars alongside the best young entrepreneurs, tours of some of the most famous innovative technology enterprises, thus fully experiencing entrepreneurship culture.

  • Highlights
  • Courses
  • - Top interactive courses: with specific topics, covering the most popular innovative technology courses, students will create their own course curriculum
  • - Immersive learning experience: live in university dormitories with students from all over the world and experience staying at an American college
  • - Classes by top instructors: Stanford Professors offer high-quality courses, giving the student insight in their academic fields of interest
  • - Discussion with Outstanding Young Entrepreneurs: Face to face talk with “Forbes 30 under 30” award-winning entrepreneurs
  • - Direct contact with entrepreneurial culture: Delve into one of the most representative American innovative companies and their knowledge base, and gain experience in entrepreneurial culture
  • - Family moment: We invite parents to participate in a camping ceremony and share the moment with their student
