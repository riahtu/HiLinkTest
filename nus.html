National University of Singapore Summer Camp

This program provides world-class financial innovation courses for young people, aged 14 to 20, wherein students will learn about business processes, in depth, and participate in youth leadership programs. Work with mentors from well-known Singaporean companies while visiting the designated wildlife protection organization, ACRES.

  • Highlights
  • Courses
  • - Take business and leadership courses at the National University of Singapore
  • - Overseas wildlife protection volunteer opportunities
  • - Youth leadership training
  • - Intern at well-known Singaporean companies (United Overseas Bank, Mediacorp, NEWwater, etc.)

Banking and Finance

  • - Learn about Singapore's financial development strategy for China and neighboring countries
  • - Sustainable financial development strategies
  • - Analysis of structural challenges and opportunities of financial markets in South East Asia

Smart City

  • - Smart Cities integrate information and communication technology
  • - Learn how physical devices connect to the IoT network
  • - Examine how efficiency of city operations and services using data analysis are optimized

Leadership and Management

  • - Leadership 101
  • - Team building
  • - Cross-cultural communication
